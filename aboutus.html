Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

Founded in 2009 by visionary entrepreneur Rajesh Anchor, Anchor Realtors began as a small real estate consultancy with a big dream: to redefine luxury living in the National Capital Region. What started with a single office in Gurugram has grown into one of the most trusted names in premium land development.

Our journey has been marked by unwavering commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. From our first project in Sector 89 to our latest ultra-premium developments, we have consistently delivered beyond expectations.

Today, Anchor Realtors stands as a testament to the power of vision, integrity, and relentless pursuit of excellence in creating spaces where families build their futures.
